# ChiGlow Splash Screen — Official Creative Direction v1.0

## Art Style
Ultra-high quality digital illustration, Apple-level premium, hyper-realistic fantasy, bright optimistic, soft cinematic lighting, modern wellness aesthetic. Vertical mobile composition. Joyful and full of life — never dark or mysterious.

- Pearl-white iridescent scales, elegant flowing translucent fins
- Graceful S-shaped swimming pose, soft intelligent expression
- Vibrant red prosperity markings (traditional Feng Shui koi)
- Represents: Prosperity, Positive Energy, Harmony, Wisdom, Healing, Transformation

## Water
- Crystal-clear bright blue, transparent aqua tones, sunlight reflections
- Sparkling highlights, dynamic flowing ribbon, white foam, bubbles
- Fresh, clean, energetic, alive — NOT dark navy or muddy
