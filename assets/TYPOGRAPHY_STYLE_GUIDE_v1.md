# ChiGlow Brand Typography & UI Style Guide v1.0
## Creative Direction for CTO — Premium Typography & Interface Refinement

### Objective
Replace flat, generic typography with elegant, harmonious brand style. Every important page should communicate warmth, calmness, elegance, and premium quality.

### Brand Philosophy
ChiGlow should feel like opening a beautifully designed wellness journal — not a business application. Every screen should feel touched by morning sunlight, gentle flowing water, graceful koi movement, lotus elegance, and calm premium craftsmanship.

### Typography Hierarchy

**Level 1 — Primary Brand Title (ChiGlow)**
- Elegant serif or custom wordmark
- Large, beautiful, premium
- Soft flowing curves, refined proportions, graceful letterforms
- Timeless rather than trendy
- Communicates calm confidence

**Level 2 — Major Page Titles**
- Home, Living Room Analysis, Harmony Journal, Membership, Color of the Day, etc.
- Refined serif font that complements ChiGlow wordmark
- Graceful while remaining highly readable

**Level 3 — Body Content**
- Existing clean modern font (Quicksand)
- Descriptions, recommendations, buttons, settings, general interface

### Decorative Flourish
- Subtle decorative flourish beneath ChiGlow wordmark
- Inspired by ChiGlow coin artwork
- Flows naturally from center, includes small lotus motif
- Symmetrical curves, clean and minimal
- Never overpowers the typography

### Buttons
- Rounded corners, soft gradients, gentle highlights, subtle depth, soft shadows
- Handcrafted rather than mechanical feel
- **Primary:** Soft Sunrise Coral gradient (already implemented)
- **Secondary:** Cream + Warm Gold Border + subtle shadow
- **Selected:** Soft Gold Glow + thin Gold Border + gentle lotus bloom animation
- No harsh outlines, no aggressive colors

### Page Headers
- Elegant title
- Optional decorative flourish
- Comfortable spacing, balanced alignment, soft breathing room
- Top of each page should immediately establish calm emotional tone

### Brand Consistency
Apply across: Home, Room Analysis, Recommendations, Color of Day, Direction of Day, Zodiac Energy, Harmony Journal, Membership, Settings, Business Analysis, Corporate Office

### Animation
- Soft fade-in, gentle upward appearance
- Tiny floating golden Chi particles nearby
- Very subtle warm lighting
- Avoid excessive animation — elegance comes from restraint

### Emotional Goal
Peace, warmth, luxury, balance, harmony. The app should feel less like software and more like a peaceful sanctuary.

### Design Philosophy
Flow. Grow. Bloom.
