# ChiGlow Design & Experience Guidelines v1.0

## Vision
ChiGlow is a calming digital sanctuary that helps users create harmony, balance, prosperity, and peace within their homes and lives.

**Emotional Goal:** "When users open ChiGlow, they should immediately feel calmer."

## Brand Identity

### Primary Brand Symbol — White Prosperity Koi
Represents: Prosperity, Positive Energy, Flow, Transformation, Wisdom, Abundance
- Pearl-white body, elegant red prosperity markings, long flowing translucent fins
- Hyper-realistic premium illustration
- Crystal-blue water, Gold Chi particles

### Secondary Brand Symbol — Gold Lotus
Represents: Harmony, Balance, Renewal, Inner Peace, Growth
- Use throughout as a subtle premium icon

### Signature Elements
Crystal Blue Chi, Golden Light, Morning Sunlight, Natural Wood, Cream Backgrounds, Green Plants, Floating Golden Particles, Elegant Water

## Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex |
|------|-------|-----|
| Background | Warm Cream | (existing) |
| Primary Heading | Rich ChiGlow Red | **#B33A35** |
| Secondary Labels | Warm Bronze Gold | **#A67C2E** |
| Body Text | Charcoal | **#4A4A4A** |
| Supporting Text | Medium Gray | **#707070** |
| Accent Gold | Icons/Badges/Decorative only | — |

**Important:** Replace yellow text with Rich ChiGlow Red (#B33A35) for headings. Use Warm Bronze Gold (#A67C2E) for labels. Never use gold/yellow for paragraphs.

## Typography Hierarchy
Heading → Bronze Label → Body Text → Supporting Text (consistent spacing)

## Illustration Style
- Premium, hyper-realistic, warm, bright, elegant, peaceful
- Morning sunlight — never dark, never gloomy
- All room illustrations: morning sunlight, natural plants, warm wood, cream colors, Blue Chi, Gold particles, subtle ChiGlow signature

### Official Illustration Collection
White Koi, Gold Koi, Pink Koi, Green Koi, Blue Koi
Splash Screen, Living Room, Bedroom, Kitchen, Bathroom, Dining Room, Office, Corporate Office, Retail Store, Restaurant/Café, Entryway, Front Yard, Backyard, Garden

### Chi Flow
Soft, transparent, wispy, glowing, natural, organic — like morning mist. Never rope, ribbon, or neon strip.

### Gold Particles
Small, floating, very subtle. Represent positive Chi. Always moving slowly.

## Motion Design
- Everything moves gently — no bouncing, no sudden animations, no flashy effects
- Card animations: softly fade upward, 10–15px, 250ms
- Button press: 98% → 100%, 150ms, very soft easing
- Lotus animation: slowly blooms → soft glow → pause → return to resting

### App Opening Sequence
Warm light fades in → Gold particles appear → White koi swims → Blue Chi flows → Gold lotus blooms → ChiGlow logo fades in → Home Screen (3–4 seconds)

### Success Screen (after scan)
Gentle sound → Blue Chi flows → Golden particles → Lotus blooms → Results fade in

### Koi Animation
Use sparingly: First Scan, Achievements, Daily Streak, Premium Welcome, Holiday Events. Slow, graceful, natural.

## Sound Design
- Single official ChiGlow signature sound
- Soft crystal singing bowl / gentle meditation bell / light wind chime / tiny shimmer
- 1.5–2 seconds — never loud, never game-like
- Play only for: App Opening, Room Scan Complete, Daily Quote, Achievement, Meditation, Daily Check-in

## Haptic Feedback
Very subtle: Button Press, Successful Scan, Achievement, Daily Check-in

## Lotus Placement
Use small gold lotus icons for: major section headings, Quick Reference, Daily Guidance, Color Coaching, Room Analysis, Harmony Sections, Premium Features, Success Messages, Achievements, Loading Screens, Empty States. Do not overuse.

## Accessibility
- Body text minimum contrast ratio: 4.5:1
- Avoid yellow text on cream backgrounds

## Performance
- Opacity, Scale, Position, light particle effects
- Lottie animations where appropriate
- Smooth on Android and iPhone

## Brand Philosophy
- White Koi = Flow
- Gold Lotus = Harmony
- Crystal Blue Water = Positive Energy
- Golden Light = Abundance
- Morning Sun = New Beginnings
- Nature = Healing

**Together: Flow. Grow. Bloom.**
Every screen, every animation, every sound, every interaction should reinforce this philosophy.
