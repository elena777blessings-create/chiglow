# 🪷 ChiGlow Design Principles

> *Not code. Vision.*

---

## 🌸 Our Promise

Every pixel, every interaction, every word in ChiGlow exists to create one feeling: **peaceful sanctuary.**

When a user opens ChiGlow, they should feel the weight of the day lift. Not because we distracted them — but because we brought them home to themselves.

---

## 🧭 The Principles

### 1. Every feature should feel calming, elegant, and intentional.

If it doesn't bring peace, it doesn't belong. No flashy animations, no urgent notifications, no noisy UI. Every element earns its place by making the experience more serene. Ask: *"Does this calm the user or agitate them?"*

### 2. Feng Shui guidance should be authentic and educational.

We are not making a game. We are sharing an ancient wisdom tradition with respect and care. Every tip, every recommendation, every element explanation should teach the user something real about Feng Shui — not just decorate the screen. Our users trust us to guide them authentically.

### 3. Visual consistency is more important than adding more features.

A beautiful, cohesive app with 10 screens is better than a cluttered app with 50. Before adding a new feature, ask: *"Does this fit our visual language?"* If it requires breaking the design system, find another way. Consistency builds trust.

### 4. The Five Elements and Bagua are the foundation of the entire app.

Wood. Fire. Earth. Metal. Water. These five elements are the grammar of our visual language. The Bagua map is the structure. Every color choice, every layout decision, every content recommendation traces back to these foundations. When in doubt, return to the elements.

### 5. The user's zodiac animal is their visual identity throughout the app.

Your zodiac animal is not just data — it's your spirit guide. It appears in the profile circle. It welcomes you on the home screen. Its energy shapes your daily reading. The animal artwork should feel personal, beautiful, and intentional — never like an afterthought.

### 6. Use generous spacing, premium typography, and nature-inspired colors.

ChiGlow breathes. Nothing feels cramped. Margins are generous. Typography follows a clear hierarchy:
- **Playfair Display** for elegant headings — warm burgundy
- **Poppins** for clean UI text — structured and modern
- **Quicksand** for affirmations and body text — soft and round

Colors come from nature, not from a UI trend report.

### 7. Red = vitality. Gold = abundance. Deep Green = harmony.

Our color palette carries meaning:
| Color | Meaning | Use |
|-------|---------|-----|
| 🔴 Rich Red (`#B33A35`) | Vitality, courage, passion | Headings, primary actions |
| 🟡 Bronze Gold (`#A67C2E`) | Abundance, warmth, wisdom | Labels, accents |
| 🌿 Deep Green (`#2E7D32`) | Harmony, growth, balance | Positive guidance text |
| 🤍 Cream (`#FFF8E7`) | Purity, sanctuary | Backgrounds |
| 💎 Crystal Blue | Positive energy | Chi effects only |

Never use pure black. Never use bright red. ChiGlow's palette is warm, aged, and natural — like a scroll in a temple, not a neon sign on a strip.

### 8. Every screen should feel peaceful.

Before shipping any screen, sit with it for 30 seconds. Take a breath. Ask yourself:
- *Does this feel calm?*
- *Would I want to look at this at the end of a long day?*
- *Does the layout guide my eye gently, or does it shout for attention?*

If the answer is anything less than "yes, it feels peaceful," keep refining.

## 🌊 Flow. Grow. Bloom.

These three words are not a tagline. They are a design philosophy:

| Word | Meaning | Design Expression |
|------|---------|-------------------|
| **Flow** | Energy moves freely | Smooth animations, generous whitespace, natural reading order |
| **Grow** | The user develops over time | Daily content, progressive features, seasonal adjustments |
| **Bloom** | Beauty emerges naturally | Elegant typography, intentional color, nature-inspired artwork |

---

## 🎨 Visual Anchors

Every screen should evoke at least one of these:

- 🐟 **White Koi** — Flow. The journey. Movement through space.
- 🪷 **Gold Lotus** — Harmony. Beauty emerging from stillness.
- 💎 **Crystal Blue Water** — Positive energy. Clarity. Purity.
- ✨ **Golden Light** — Abundance. Warmth. Illumination.
- 🌅 **Morning Sun** — New beginnings. Hope. Fresh starts.
- 🌳 **Nature** — Healing. Grounding. Connection to the earth.

---

## 🚫 What We Do Not Do

- We do not use urgency or pressure tactics ("Only 3 spots left!")
- We do not use harsh contrasts or bright neon colors
- We do not clutter screens with too much information
- We do not gamify Feng Shui — it is not a game
- We do not make promises we cannot ground in real knowledge
- We do not ship features that break the visual system

---

## 💫 The Ultimate Test

When you open ChiGlow, you should feel calmer than you did before.

If the app does not pass this test, go back and refine.
